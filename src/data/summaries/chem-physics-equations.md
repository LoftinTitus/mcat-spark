## Physics/Chemistry Summary Equations

### General Chemistry
- **Ideal Gas Law**: \( PV = nRT \)
- **Combined Gas Law**: \( \frac{P_1V_1}{T_1} = \frac{P_2V_2}{T_2} \)
- **Boyle's Law**: \( P \propto \frac{1}{V} \) (at constant T)
- **Charles's Law**: \( V \propto T \) (at constant P)
- **Avogadro's Law**: \( V \propto n \) (at constant P, T)
- **Rate Law**: \( \text{Rate} = k[A]^m[B]^n \)
- **Equilibrium Constant**: \( K = \frac{[products]^{coeff}}{[reactants]^{coeff}} \)
- **Nernst Equation**: \( E = E^0 - \frac{RT}{nF} \ln Q \)

### Physics
- **Kinematics**:
  \[ v = v_0 + at \]
  \[ x = x_0 + v_0t + \frac{1}{2}at^2 \]
  \[ v^2 = v_0^2 + 2a(x - x_0) \]
- **Newton's Second Law**: \( F = ma \)
- **Work-Energy Theorem**: \( W = \Delta KE \)
- **Conservation of Energy**: \( KE_i + PE_i = KE_f + PE_f \)
- **Coulomb's Law**: \( F = k_e \frac{q_1q_2}{r^2} \)
- **Ohm's Law**: \( V = IR \)
- **Power in Circuits**: \( P = IV \)
- **Wave Equation**: \( v = f\lambda \)
- **Snell's Law**: \( n_1\sin\theta_1 = n_2\sin\theta_2 \)
- **Lens Equation**: \( \frac{1}{f} = \frac{1}{d_o} + \frac{1}{d_i} \)

### Thermodynamics
- **First Law**: \( \Delta U = q + w \)
- **Gibbs Free Energy**: \( \Delta G = \Delta H - T\Delta S \)
- **Relationship to K**: \( \Delta G^0 = -RT \ln K \)
- **Entropy Change**: \( \Delta S = \frac{q_{rev}}{T} \)

### Acids and Bases
- **pH**: \( pH = -\log[H^+] \)
- **Henderson-Hasselbalch**: \( pH = pKa + \log\frac{[A^-]}{[HA]} \)
